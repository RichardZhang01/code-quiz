Coding Quiz Challenge

Welcome to the Coding Quiz Challenge. This is a timed quiz. When you click the start button, you will be presented with a series of 10 questions, and you will be given 60 seconds to answer all of them, as indicated by the timer in the top right. If you answer a question correctly, no time will be deducted from the timer. If you answer a question incorrectly, 10 seconds will be deducted from the timer. When all questions are answered or the timer reaches 0, the quiz is over and your score will be displayed. You will be given the option to save your initials and your score once the quiz is over.
